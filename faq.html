Common Questions About
Our Services

  • Do you offer free vehicle pickup?
    Yes! SKY Auto Parts provides a 100% free pick-up service for unwanted, damaged, or old vehicles within the Manawatu Region and nearby areas.
  • Do you buy used car parts from customers?
    Absolutely. We buy quality second-hand car parts from customers. Get in touch with us to receive a quick evaluation and offer.
  • Can I visit your yard and pull parts myself?
    Yes, customers are welcome to visit our salvage yard, locate the parts they need, and remove them on-site. It’s an affordable and hands-on way to get what you need.
  • Do you offer warranties on used parts?
    We carefully inspect all parts before sale. While most parts are sold as-is, we do offer limited warranties on selected items. Please ask our staff for details.
  • What areas do you service?
    We are based in Palmerston North and provide service across the Manawatu Region including Levin and surrounding suburbs.
  • Can I order parts online or by phone?
    Yes, you can call us directly at 0800 149 419 or email us to inquire about availability. We’ll help you find the right part quickly and easily.
